About Me

I am a TRCN-certified educator with dual specialities in engineering and pedagogy, I leverage technology to create learning opportunities rather than focusing on building or producing tools. I integrate engineering problem-solving and research-based teaching methodologies into a structured learning path, which bridges students' theoretical knowledge with the application of that knowledge in the real world.

In addition to utilising technology to provide students with effective, efficient educational experiences, by applying Python and Data Analytics to automate administrative processes and create evidence-based insights into student experiences and performance and to turn complex data into actionable insights about students, teachers, classrooms, and schools. I am also involved in curriculum research and development, educator training, and promoting best practices through the sharing of resources. Furthermore, I continually learn about educational technologies and implement best practices in this area.
